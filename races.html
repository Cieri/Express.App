Humans

Humans are the main inhabitants of Lutos and Atoth and have remained the group in power since the earliest records. Human civilizations were slower to develop in the beginning because of infighting, but once the societies became horticultural, they developed at a rapid pace, constructing sprawling cities and inventing new technologies. Now a primarily agricultural race, they occupy and rule much of the territory on both of the main continents.

Elves & Wild Elves

Elves are thought to have originated from Fokos, The Lost Realm, but much of their recorded history is bare. The elves that live on Lutos and Atoth are divided into two distinct groups. The elves who purposely avoid humans call themselves “Free Elves” and have long, sharp ears. In Lutos, they are often found in the Cursed Forest, a place humans have no interest in colonizing. Believing violence to be a human quality, these elves eschew weaponry and war as well as hunting and consuming their animal-brethren. The elves that live amongst the humans have shorter, rounder ears than the Free Elves, and they do not give themselves a special name. These elves, as well as the humans they comingle with, refer to the Free Elves as “Wild Elves”, believing them to be barbaric fanatics.
