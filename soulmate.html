Soulthreads

Every mortal is born with a thread-like pattern unique to them and their destined match. Free Elves believe this to be a gift from their gods for the virtue they exhibit, and they do not believe this blessing is extended to humans. Soulthreads are only visible to their owner unless a soulbond is completed, in which case soulmates can see each other’s soulthreads.

Soulbonds

If a destined pair are in extremely close proximity, the soulthreads will begin to itch, as if trying to alert their owners. If the soulmates make any form of skin-to-skin contact, the soulthreads will connect, thus completing the soulbond.

Soulsharing

In a completed soulbond, soulmates can sense their mate’s emotions through the bond if the emotion is strong or urgent enough and if the soulmates are near enough to each other. Soulsharing is meant to bring the mates closer. While it is possible to muffle these sensations, it is impossible to block them completely.
